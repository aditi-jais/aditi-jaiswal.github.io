Projects

Drum Kit

This project offers an immersive drumming experience in a virtual environment. Users can engage with the drum kit by either clicking on the drum buttons displayed on the screen or by using their computer keyboard to trigger drum sounds. This interactivity provides users with the freedom to choose their preferred method of drumming and caters to different user preferences.
In addition to the drum buttons, the project also includes music buttons that enable users to create and play back predefined patterns. By clicking on these music buttons, users can trigger a sequence of drum sounds that are programmed to play in a specific order. This feature adds a layer of creativity and musicality to the drum kit experience, allowing users to experiment with different rhythms and beats.
The project offers customization options through the speed and loop controls. The speed control allows users to adjust the tempo or playback speed of the drum sounds. By sliding the range input, users can set the speed to their desired tempo, whether it be slow and steady or fast-paced and energetic. The loop control, on the other hand, allows users to determine the number of times the predefined patterns will be played. By adjusting the loop count, users can create repetitive sequences or set a single playback for a concise musical arrangement.

Simon Game

The project, known as the Simon game, is designed to be an enjoyable and interactive experience that helps players enhance their memory and pattern recognition skills. By accurately repeating the sequences of colored buttons and sounds in the Simon game, players can advance to higher levels and aim for better scores. The game's visuals and sounds create an immersive experience that keeps players engaged and entertained.
As players progress through the levels in the Simon game, the challenges become more demanding, requiring them to concentrate and rely on their memory. The buttons' colors fading in and out provide visual cues that make the Simon game dynamic and exciting.
The Simon game is easy to play, allowing players to press any keys to start then click the colored buttons on the screen to play.
